Installation

Pre-requisite: Make sure you have installed the drivers for the Wemos d1 to communicate with your pc / laptop. Look for it at the troubleshooting page.
And you must connect the esp to your pc or laptop with a cable that transfers both power and data.
You can use the button below to install the tools firmware directly to your device via USB from the browser.
